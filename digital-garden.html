Digital Garden (Under Construction)


The World Wide Web has always fascinated me. I feel as though I grew up on the Internet, and that it has taught me a lot of what I know. While the home page is built as my "professional website", mostly for clients and mostly for showcasing my work, this section of the website will be my personal wiki and digital garden. I've put all my hard work onto this website – it has been almost 301 days since I started on creating this, experimenting since I am not really a professional in computer science or web development (whatever you call it). I'm just a hobbyist, and I enjoy coding but also appreciate the #NoCode culture. While the website is not particularly flashing or interactive enough, I do believe that readability, emphasizing content and accessibility is the most important aspect of any website.

The only goal I had in mind when I built this part of the website was to continue to share ideas and insights with everyone; offline or online.

This website is very simple, built primarily in HTML and CSS. It is hosted in Github and distributed globally via Cloudflare. And it only produces 0.12g of CO2 whenever someone visits the site. I am actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of my website to everyone (and me).

I'm just starting off on this digital garden journey so it may take a while. I am not good with words, but I try. Amping kanunay! (Take care always!)
