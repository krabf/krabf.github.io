Kushaiah Felisilda

Kushaiah Felisilda is a Filmmaker and Photographer based in the Philippines. She first developed her fondness for films and video works by scouring her parent’s wide array of DVDs.

With a Bachelor’s Degree in Cinema from the University of San Carlos, she has competent knowledge and experience not only with the technical requirements but also the theories behind the production processes of a film. She has worked with various projects both for film and commercial purposes locally and internationally. She also loves nerding out about productivity apps.

Kushaiah’s knowledge and skillsets are acquired from self-experimentation and learned ideas passed onto by people she has collaborated with before. She believes that one of the most important tools in ensuring the success of a project is effective communication — both sides must have a mutual, shared understanding of all agreements and decisions in pursuit of the representation of one’s values, ideas, mission, and history.
